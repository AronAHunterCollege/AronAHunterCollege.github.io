I'm an emerging media major. I decided to take up coding in general because I wanted to work in gaming. However, I also knew that the industry was volatile and success of any sort uncertain. So, I decided to broaden my skill set in order to make sure I was well rounded enough to ensure financial stability. Or at least, that's the plan. Who's to say it'll actually work out. But, if nothing else, this is a genuine field of interest to me. I've never been interested in coding from a typical computer science standpoint, but rather how it can serve to provide content people care about. It's my hope that in whatever industry I end up in, I can make things that people enjoy and remember in any capacity.
